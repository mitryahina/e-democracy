Timeline, 1984-1986

Petro Poroshenko defended a thesis on the topic "Legal regulation of the management of state corporate rights in Ukraine" at the Odessa Law Academy. And started to career in people's deputy field.

Headed parliamentary committees on budget and finance.

He was appointed to the post of Secretary of the National Security and Defense Council.

Poroshenko is the head of the NBU Council.

Foreign minister.

Minister of Economic Development and Trade of Ukraine.

Participated in the 2014 President election, where he received 54.7% of the vote, winning the first round.
